About Rail Hunt

Rail Hunt is an engaging strategy game that challenges players to build efficient train networks to transport resources across the map. As a railway tycoon, your mission is to create a profitable transportation empire by connecting cities, managing trains, and optimizing routes.

The game combines elements of resource management, strategic planning, and economic simulation. Each decision you make affects your railway's growth and profitability. Will you focus on passenger transport for quick returns, or invest in freight networks for long-term gains?

With its intuitive interface and progressively challenging levels, Rail Hunt offers hours of engaging gameplay for both casual gamers and strategy enthusiasts. Put your planning skills to the test and see if you have what it takes to build the ultimate rail empire!

Tips & Tricks

Plan Your Routes Carefully

Before laying tracks, survey the map to identify key resource locations and cities. The most efficient networks connect multiple points with minimal track length.

Pro tip: Use tunnels and bridges strategically to overcome geographical obstacles rather than building long detours.

Balance Your Budget

Track maintenance costs can accumulate quickly. Focus on profitable routes first and expand gradually as your revenue increases.

Pro tip: Passenger routes between large cities often provide steady income to fund more extensive freight operations.

Upgrade Strategically

Don't rush to buy the fastest trains immediately. Upgrade your busiest routes first and ensure your infrastructure can support higher speeds.

Pro tip: Sometimes multiple smaller trains are more efficient than one large train, especially when serving multiple destinations along a route.
